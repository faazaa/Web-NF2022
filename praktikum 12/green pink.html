Karina (카리나)
is a South Korean singer and rapper under SM Entertainment. She is the leader of the girl group aespa and a member of the female unit GOT the beat.
Birth date
April 11, 2000 (age 22), Bundang-gu, Seongnam, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea
2019: Pre-debut
On February 2019, Karina appeared in Taemin's "Want" music video and also acted as backup dancer for the song's promotional stages.
2020: Debut with aespa
On October 28, 2020, Karina was revealed as the second member of aespa.[3][4] The group debuted on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba".

Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. Debuted on November 17, 2020, with the single "Black Mamba".
